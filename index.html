Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Nitro Play Games ("we", "our", or "us") operates mobile games available on the Google Play Store. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our games and services.

1. Information We Collect

Our games may collect the following types of information:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers)
  • Gameplay data (scores, progress, in-game actions)
  • Advertising data via third-party ad networks such as Google AdMob
  • Crash and diagnostic data to improve game performance

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without parental consent.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve our games and services
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyse gameplay to fix bugs and improve performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Third-Party Services

Our games may include third-party services such as Google AdMob (advertising), Google Play Services (leaderboards, achievements), and Firebase (analytics and crash reporting). These services have their own privacy policies.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated, non-identifiable data for analytics purposes.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our games are not directed at children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
